About the Root

I specialize in licorice root exports to Europe and other regions of the world. Licorice root, which grows in Azerbaijan, is considered one of the highest quality in the world. Licorice root from Azerbaijan contains an increased amount of glycyrrhizic acid. This feature and excellent flavor makes it especially valuable in the food, tobacco, and alcoholic beverage industries.
